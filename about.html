About AdeaPmd CareerConnect

We empower culinary arts graduates by transforming their skills into real-world opportunities, and we change students' lives through fees assistance and mentorship.

Who We Are

AdeaPmd CareerConnect is dedicated to nurturing culinary talent and supporting students who may struggle financially. Our mission goes beyond skills training—we help students pursue their dreams by providing financial assistance, mentorship, and career guidance.

Our Mission

To connect culinary arts graduates with opportunities, mentorship, and financial support that transforms lives and turns passion into sustainable careers.

Our Vision

To be a leading career bridge for vocational graduates across Kenya and beyond, shaping confident professionals ready for the hospitality industry.

What Makes Us Different

Skill-Focused

We highlight practical skills and real abilities, not just certificates.

Life-Changing Support

We provide fee assistance to deserving students to help them continue their education.

Job Connections

Direct access to culinary job opportunities and internships.

Mentorship

Guidance from experienced trainers and industry professionals.
